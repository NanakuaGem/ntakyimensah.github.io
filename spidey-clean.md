# Go-to-Market Strategy for a Concept Product at iRobot

## Overview
This project develops a full marketing strategy for **Spidey Clean**, a concept robotic cleaning product designed to clean spider webs and hard-to-reach areas. The goal was to explore whether a niche wall-climbing cleaning robot could be a viable product extension for iRobot.

---

## Problem
Most robotic cleaners focus on floor cleaning, leaving elevated and hard-to-reach surfaces largely unaddressed.

---

## Solution
Spidey Clean is a wall-climbing robotic cleaner designed to remove spider webs and debris from ceilings, walls, and elevated spaces.  

**Key Differentiators:**
- Wall-climbing capability  
- Specialized cleaning attachments  
- Safety-first design  
- Backed by iRobot brand trust

---

## Analysis & Insights
- **SWOT Analysis:** Strengths include brand reputation and technological leadership; opportunities include smart-home adoption and niche cleaning needs.  
- **Financial Projections:** Year 1 revenue: $3.6M → Year 5 revenue: $9.8M  
- **Marketing Strategy:** Skimming pricing, influencer marketing, omnichannel distribution
